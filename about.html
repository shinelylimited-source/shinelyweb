What's Included in Our General Cleaning?

Our comprehensive general cleaning service covers all essential areas of your property, ensuring nothing is overlooked. Here's what you can expect:

  • Dusting All surfaces, fixtures, baseboards, and hard-to-reach areas
  • Vacuuming & Mopping All floors, carpets, and hard surfaces
  • Bathroom Sanitization Toilets, sinks, showers, tubs, mirrors, and fixtures
  • Kitchen Cleaning Countertops, appliances, sinks, and cabinet exteriors
  • Trash Removal Emptying all bins and replacing liners
  • Window Cleaning Interior windows and sills (exterior available)

Our General Cleaning Services

Comprehensive cleaning solutions for every need

Residential Cleaning

Regular housekeeping services including dusting, vacuuming, mopping, bathroom sanitization, and kitchen cleaning. Keep your home fresh and inviting with our reliable weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly schedules.

Commercial Cleaning

Professional office cleaning that creates a productive work environment. We handle desks, common areas, restrooms, break rooms, and high-touch surfaces to maintain a pristine workplace.

Deep Cleaning

Intensive cleaning that goes beyond surface level. We tackle baseboards, inside appliances, behind furniture, grout, and hard-to-reach areas for a truly thorough clean.

Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning

End of tenancy cleaning that ensures you get your deposit back or walk into a spotless new home. We clean every corner so you can focus on your move.

Airbnb & Short-Term Rental

Fast turnaround cleaning between guests. We ensure your property is guest-ready with fresh linens, sanitized surfaces, and that welcoming first impression.

Industrial & Warehouse

Heavy-duty cleaning for warehouses, factories, and industrial facilities. Floor scrubbing, high-reach dusting, equipment cleaning, and safety compliance.
